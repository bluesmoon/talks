Device Widths

  • iPad: 1024 & 768
  • iPhone/iPod: 480 & 320
  • Retina displays double that
  • Android devices are sometimes the same and sometimes different
  • My phone is 160px wide
  • The big graph dashboard screen is way bigger

What can you target

  • min/max-width / device-width
  • orientation (but don't bother)
  • pixel density, aspect-ratio
  • media type (print, screen, projection, braille, tv, etc.)
  • and more...

Best to always look at specific widths, since pages scroll vertically. Test.
